The Journey

changing · evolving · growing

The Blog Era

Two college passouts, one keyboard

Fresh out of college, Rohit Singh and Krishan Kumar built a successful blog powered by SEO. The traffic flowed, the income was great, and life was good. Until it wasn't.

The Storm

Google's core update wipes it all out

One algorithm change. Traffic collapsed overnight. Most people would call it an ending — Rohit and Krishan refused to. They took the hardest lesson in digital business early: never build your house on rented land.

2017

Million Cases is born

India was upgrading to premium smartphones, but premium protection was nowhere to be found. The duo pivoted into eCommerce and launched Million Cases — quality-obsessed cases and accessories for people who love their phones.

2018

Millions Kart Private Limited

The parent company is incorporated in Punjab. Not an agency, not a reseller — a brand-building machine with its own warehouse, in-house tools, and a squad that ships.

The Expansion

Peeperly joins the family

Millions of women wanted accessories that were pretty and protective — the market shrugged, so we built it. Peeperly launched with designer cases across marble, florals and mandala art, and grew into a 350,000+ strong community. Boldacc followed for fashion-tech accessories.

Today

₹120Cr+ sales · 2M+ orders · 6 brands

From a single blog to one of North India's proudest D2C stories — powered by our own brand squad, our own systems, and values we refuse to compromise. And honestly? We're just warming up.

"Neglecting profitability and blindly pursuing sales is akin to self-sabotage."
